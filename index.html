Bio

My name is Javier Trevizo and I was born and raised in Southern California. I am currently a senior at the University of California Irvine. I'm majoring in Informatics with a specialization in Organizations and Information Technology(OIT). In 2015 my team got second place at the quartlery VDGC Game Jam for our game Guesstimate, which was made using Unity. We went on to further develop our game and took first place at the IEEE Computer Game Showcase later that year. I'm an avid PC Gamer and my most played game is League of Legends. I'm a, currently, Gold 3 Karma main and typically go jungle, mid, or support.
